Why use Gear Box?

Gear Box is great for travel or just hanging out; we've got a variety of users with gear available for you to rent. Save exorbitant airline fees for traveling with large items such as kayaks, surfboards, and snowboards. Now you can live out that life long dream of going on a combination motorcycle kayaking trip!

Safe & Secure

We handle payment through Stripe, making credit card payments easy. The lender charges an appropriate deposit for their item, to be returned once the item is received back in good condition. Users are encouraged to use our feedback system to help support a thriving rental community.
